## 常见后缀

| 后缀 | 含义/用法 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| -tion/-sion | 行为，状态 | education, decision |
| -ment | 行为，结果 | development, movement |
| -ness | 性质，状态 | happiness, kindness |
| -ity | 性质，状态 | ability, reality |
| -er/-or | 做...的人 | teacher, actor |
| -ist | ...家，...者 | scientist, artist |
| -ism | 主义，学说 | tourism, optimism |
| -ance/-ence | 性质，状态 | importance, difference |

| -en | 使... | strengthen, widen |

## 记忆技巧

1. **词根联想**: 记住 spect = 看 → inspect (往里看=检查), respect (反复看=尊敬)
2. **前缀定位**: 看到 un-/dis-/in- 先想"否定"
3. **后缀辨词性**: -tion/-ment 是名词，-ful/-less 是形容词
4. **组合记忆**: international = inter (相互) + nation (国家) + al (的) = 国际的
